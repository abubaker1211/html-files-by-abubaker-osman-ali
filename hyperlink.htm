White Rice

Steps to cook White Rice

Ingredients:

1️⃣White rice
2️⃣Water
3️⃣Salt (optional)

Equipment:

1️⃣Medium-sized saucepan with a lid
2️⃣Spoon or fork for fluffing


Step 1
Rinse the Rice (Optional but recommended):
Place the rice in a fine-mesh sieve or bowl.
Rinse the rice under cold water, swirling it around with your hand or stirring gently. This helps remove excess starch and any debris.
Continue rinsing until the water runs clear. This step can improve the texture of the cooked rice.

Step 2
Measure the Rice and Water:
Use the ratio of 1:2 for rice to water, meaning for every cup of rice, use 2 cups of water. Adjust this ratio based on your preference for softer or firmer rice.

Step 3
Combine Rice and Water in the Saucepan:
Place the rinsed rice into the saucepan.
Add the measured water to the saucepan. If desired, add a pinch of salt for seasoning (optional).

Step 4
Bring to a Boil:
Place the saucepan on the stove over medium-high heat.
Bring the water and rice to a boil. Stir once to ensure the rice isn't sticking to the bottom.

Step 5
Simmer:
Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the saucepan with a lid.
Let the rice simmer for about 15-20 minutes. Avoid lifting the lid during this time to ensure even cooking and to retain steam.

Step 6
Check for Doneness:
After about 15 minutes, check if the rice is tender and the water has been absorbed. If not, cover and cook for a few more minutes. Be careful not to overcook the rice.

Step 7
Fluff and Serve:
Once done, remove the saucepan from the heat.
Let the rice sit covered for a couple of minutes to steam.
Fluff the rice gently with a fork or spoon to separate the grains.

Step 8
Serve:
Serve the rice as a side dish or as part of your favorite recipe.
