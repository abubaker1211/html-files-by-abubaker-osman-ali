Bollywood Celebs

SHAH RUKH KHAN

Shah Rukh Khan, often referred to as the "King of Bollywood," has had a career spanning several decades. Born on November 2nd, 1965, in New Delhi, India, he initially started with television before making a successful transition to films. Known for his romantic roles and charismatic persona, Shah Rukh Khan has starred in numerous blockbuster hits like "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge," "Kuch Kuch Hota Hai," and "Chennai Express."

Despite a few setbacks, his box office appeal remains strong, making him one of the highest-paid actors in India. He is also known for his philanthropic efforts and ownership of the IPL cricket team Kolkata Knight Riders.

DEEPIKA PADUKONE

Deepika Padukone, born on January 5th, 1986, in Copenhagen, Denmark, to badminton player Prakash Padukone, is one of the highest-paid actresses in Bollywood. She made her debut in 2007 with "Om Shanti Om" opposite Shah Rukh Khan and quickly rose to fame for her versatile acting skills and graceful demeanor.

Deepika has been part of several commercially successful films such as "Chennai Express," "Padmaavat," and "Bajirao Mastani." Alongside her acting career, she is actively involved in advocating for mental health awareness and empowerment of women.
